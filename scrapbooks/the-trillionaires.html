The Trillionaires

They just showed up one day, the three Trillionaires. Then they were everywhere: on CNN, in Lego toys, in the Sears Wishbook catalog. Although you'd never see them in person they dominated the mainstream culture. They'd always appear in emoji form. But there was resistance. One of the more visible forms was the graffiti made with the standard Lego sets distributed to children. Some examples were collected in this show in the AS220 Resident Gallery in 2024:

  2. Next Level 3d Money Laundering
  3. Stinktanks: a Fungus Among Us
  4. War Profiteering for fun and Profit
  5. Total Information Awareness
  6. Bunker Mode
  7. Ambushing Institutions With Acid and Urine
