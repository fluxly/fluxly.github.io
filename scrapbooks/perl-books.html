1998-2003

Perl Books for O'Reilly

In 1998 I wrote Programming Web Graphics. It's about how to write programs that generate graphics destined for the web. It was published by O'Reilly in early 1999.

Perl Graphics Programming was published in 2003 and shows how to generate GIFs, JPEGs, PNGs, SVGs, and PDFs using Perl and free software. Also SWF (Flash) files!

I also contributed three chapters to the Perl Resource Kit in 1997.
