Solo Music

For a couple of years I was playing mandolin and accordion every Friday before showings at the Cable Car Cinema. I've also done a few stints as the in-between music for the Blood From a Turnip Late Night Puppet Salon, and for years played weekly at the Pork Chop Lounge at AS220.
